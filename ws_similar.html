This is the first edition of the Workshop on Argumentation for Explanations. While no other workshop on the same topic has been proposed so far, a few other events proposed in different venues emphasize a broad and increasing research interest in related topics (argument mining, computational models of argumentation, explainability), for instance:

  • The ArgMining workshop series devoted to argument mining is at its 10th edition (co-located with main ACL events, in 2023 with EMNLP)
  • The Conference on Computational Models of Argument (COMMA) at its 9th edition
  • The ArgXAI: 1st and 2nd edition of the International Workshop on Argumentation for eXplainable AI co-located with COMMA22 and COMMA24
  • The Natural Language Reasoning and Structured Explanations Workshop co-located with ACL 2023
  • The IJCAI 2023
